Meet the Justice League Members

Superman

Image credit: Wikimedia Commons

Superman

Superman, also known as the Man of Steel, possesses incredible powers, including superhuman strength, flight, invulnerability, super speed, heat vision, and x-ray vision. His alter ego is Clark Kent, a mild-mannered reporter for the Daily Planet. Superman is often considered as the world's greatest hero, he is often depicted as the most powerful memer of the Justice league alongside characters such as Martian Manhunter and Wonder Woman.

Image credit: Wikimedia Commons

Batman

Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, possesses genius level intellect across many scientifc fields, is a master of 127 fighting styles such as Boxing, Wing chun, Taekwando, Wrestling, Kickboxing, Karate and many others. Over the years he has honed his body to the physical human peak - so much so that he is often mistaken for being superhuman. Whilst he may not have superhuman abilities, he is a master of stealth & intimidation and uses both to effectively battle crime lords and supervillain's alike.

Martian Manhunter

Image credit: Wikimedia Commons

Martian Manhunter

Martian Manhunter, also known as J'onn J'onzz is the sole survivor of his home planet Mars, his powers including telepathy, shape-shifting, intangibility, and invisibility. He boats one of the most versatile power sets in all of comics making him a formidable foe, amazing teammate and essential member of the league.

Wonder Woman

Image credit: Wikimedia Commons

Wonder Woman

Wonder Woman, princess Diana of Themyscira, is equipped with superhuman strength, agility, durability, centuries of combat experiance and the Lasso of Truth. Diana is often considered as the big powerhouse next to Superman and Martian Manhunter. She has been stated to be the best fighter in the wolrd by Batman himself.

The Flash

Image credit: Wikimedia Commons

The Flash

Barry Allen, also known as The Flash, is the fastest man alive! He uses his speed to run at lightspeed, manipulate time and space, create lightning based attacks and fight crime in the blink of an eye. A gifted forensics scientist rivaling the detective skills of Batman t times, Barry is a pivotal part of the team and often provides essential recon, crowd evacuation and power to the team.
